Shanghai Cultural Industry Development Report 2019 released

The year 2019 marks the 70th anniversary of the founding of New China, the sprint year for the completion of a moderately prosperous society and the implementation of the 13th Five-Year Plan, as well as the key year for the implementation of the three new major tasks assigned to Shanghai by General Secretary Xi Jinping. In accordance with the overall plan of the Central Government, Shanghai's cultural industry continued to implement the "Several Opinions on Accelerating the Innovative Development of the City's Cultural and Creative Industries" ("Shanghai Cultural and Creative 50"), in accordance with the national strategy and the actual development of Shanghai, comprehensively implement the new development concept, implement the requirements of high-quality development, and focus on promoting the global film and television production centre, art exchange centre, Asian performance centre, and the Asian cultural and creative industries. The city will also take the opportunity to promote the integrated and higher quality development of the Yangtze River Delta and the comprehensive deepening of the reform and opening up of the Pilot Free Trade Zone, and give full play to its advantages of "source" and "dock". "We will continue to deepen the reform, improve the service environment, perfect the policy guarantee, strengthen the market players, enhance the industrial capacity and core competitiveness, promote the deep integration of culture and technology, actively cultivate new business models, transform the cultural development mode, further strengthen the city's cultural soft power and international influence, and effectively enhance the people's This will provide a solid foundation and strong guarantee for the comprehensive branding of "Shanghai Culture" and accelerate the construction of an international cultural metropolis.

In order to let the public understand the development of Shanghai's cultural industry in 2019, the Cultural Reform and Development Office of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, the Shanghai Cultural Business Administration and the Institute of Humanities and Arts of Shanghai Jiaotong University have jointly compiled the "2019 Shanghai Cultural Industry Development Report". This report mainly analyzes the basic data of Shanghai's cultural industry in 2018[1], summarizes the current situation and main features of the development of Shanghai's cultural industry in 2019, and makes an analysis and judgment on the future development prospects of Shanghai's cultural industry.
