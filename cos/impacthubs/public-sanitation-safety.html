Public Sanitation Safety

Background

Since the outbreak of the novel coronavirus in 2019, the novel coronavirus has been raging around the world and has never disappeared. According to experts, human beings will coexist with COVID-19 for a long time. This also means that when the epidemic breaks out, blocking the city is not a long-term solution. If mankind wants to develop, it needs to be open. How to ensure people's safety without affecting people's life and economic development is a problem worthy of discussion.

Shanghai is located in the east of China, the estuary of the Yangtze River and the East China Sea. With excellent natural conditions and convenient transportation, Shanghai, which plays an important role in China, is the most economically developed city in China. It is known as the world's largest port and an important port participating in the international economic cycle.

However, it is precisely because of Shanghai's developed economy, convenient transportation, dense population and frequent personnel mobility that Shanghai has also brought great challenges to epidemic prevention and resistance. As the most economically developed city in China, Shanghai has been responding quickly, setting a good example for the whole of China. The epidemic will eventually dissipate, but the sudden major public safety accident of the epidemic has sounded an alarm to the whole world. It is very important to race against time to promote the transformation and upgrading of public health security and seek development while guarding the defense line of people's security.

Aim

· Promote the transformation and upgrading of public health in Shanghai, and gradually build and upgrade the health barrier in China with Shanghai as the center;

· Carry out public health sharing expert meetings to promote international cooperation and build a community with a shared future for mankind.

Main points

· Build a contingent of high-level public health professionals;

· Strengthen the awareness of risk prevention, and coordinate development and safety;

· Strengthen public health security and international trade security.

The Public Sanitation Safety Prevention Plan

In April 2020, Shanghai released policy “several opinions about complete major epidemic prevention and control mechanism and improve the public health emergency management system ", the report points out that by the year 2025, Shanghai will reach the international level and make Shanghai become one of the safest cities in a global public health while facing public health emergency.

The Public Sanitation Safety Prevention Plan, which is drafted by related experts, is to provide a set of key guidance and polices to make the goals come true. The plan is not only to promote the upgrading and development of Shanghai's public health security, but also to promote international cooperation in health, and establish and strengthen the awareness of a community with a shared future for mankind.

The Public Sanitation Safety Prevention Partnership

The Public Sanitation Safety Prevention Partnership (PSS2P) is a new group, initiated by The Club of Shanghai and cooperate with Shanghai Public Health Center. It brings together more than 100 partners form across the Anthropology, epidemiology and sociology, including scientists, doctors, Sociologists, government officials and business leaders. PSS2P is planning to organize a Youth Reserve Department (YRD). Therefore, we welcome people from different research backgrounds to join us.
