Let the industrial brand, city brand and cultural brand rise together

The achievements of China's urbanisation are clear to all. But we also feel deeply that, compared to urbanisation, we have not yet reached the ideal state in terms of cultural heritage and innovation, and the integration of architecture and poetry, which has brought about a lack of cultural identity and a sense of belonging to the city, which is a pity. How can we avoid this regret? Fengxian has been exploring this. We insist on the integration of ecology, architecture, industry and people, and make it our endeavour to form an exchange platform where the atmosphere is vivid and the life is mutual. When we travel through all the beautiful cities in the world, we are always attracted by their stunning landscapes, classic architecture, individual industries and livable spaces. And Fengxian has its own unique temperament in these aspects as well. Located on the East China Sea and Hangzhou Bay, Fengxian has an ecological space with access to the river and the sea, a world of trees, a sea of flowers, a paradise for birds, a home of clouds and a paradise for people; it has the "Great Wall of the Sea", one of the three major municipal buildings in ancient China, which is a key cultural heritage currently being excavated and developed by the Shanghai Municipal Government and Fengxian District Government. The "Oriental Beauty Valley" is the capital of China's cosmetic industry, with its cosmetic, food and pharmaceutical industries, and it is also the centre of South Shanghai according to the Shanghai 2035 Master Plan. We have always insisted on transforming industrial brands into city brands, and then into cultural brands, to achieve the joint ascension of industrial brands, city brands and cultural brands. In the next step, we will push forward in depth in industrial culture, city culture and city branding, and strive to create a multi-dimensional space for city culture.
