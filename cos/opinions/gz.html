Emergency drills should be further strengthened to ensure effective response

Timely activation of the emergency response mechanism is the key to strictly controlling the consequences of a major epidemic. Therefore, emergency measures should be further refined. At the same time, it is necessary to fundamentally solve the phenomenon that the emergency plan is not useful, the upper and lower are generally rough, and even put into the shelf, so as to improve the quality and operability of the emergency plan. In addition, emergency drills should be further strengthened to ensure effective response and orderly operation under abnormal conditions. Practice shows that, if not at ordinary times, urgent must show incompetence. Major epidemic outbreaks, tend to be a huge impact on the economic and social operation, the effective mechanism of normal may lose normal function, this makes establish emergency response mechanism to improve national governance system and the promotion of the important content of management ability, if no emergency mechanism necessary exercise at ordinary times, this mechanism can't work after the outbreak of an epidemic. Therefore, strengthening emergency drills under normal conditions can not only update the emergency response mechanism with The Times, but also enhance people's vigilance against major epidemics.
