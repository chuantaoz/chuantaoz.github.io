About Club of Shanghai

The club of shanghai was established to study the multiple crises Shanghai faces in cultural industry, population, environment, and public safety. With the joint efforts of our 4 members (graduate students from different professional backgrounds), we seek to specify a comprehensive solution to the complex, contradictory, and interrelated challenges in the city of Shanghai.

The birth rate has fallen in the past few years and the degree of aging has deepened. As a window to China, Shanghai has a rich traditional cultural status. At the same time, public safety issues are becoming more and more prominent in Shanghai. The proposal of a multi-faceted reform plan for Shanghai is imminent.

As a member network with a wealth of professional knowledge and ideas, our members facilitate difficult interviews and propose bold ideas for dealing with these social issues. Our goal is to look at the problems in Shanghai from a new perspective, and to promote pilot projects in Shanghai and major cities with these problems through more innovative solutions, so that Shanghai can get rid of the current crisis and provide a model for other cities. To prevent these problems from happening again.

With the help of scientific analysis, the club of shanghai put forward suggestions to solve these huge and far-reaching problems. It achieves this goal through research, specific policy recommendations, and member meetings, debates and other activities. It also released 4 impact hub research reports. Recently, the club listed 4 impact hubs as priorities: Public Sanitation Safety, Carbon Emission, Low Birth Rate, Cultural Industry.
