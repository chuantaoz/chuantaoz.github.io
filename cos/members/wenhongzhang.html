Wenhong Zhang

Wenhong Zhang, male, born in Rui'an, Zhejiang Province in August 1969. He has been engaged in clinical research of infectious diseases and liver diseases for a long time. Chief physician, doctoral supervisor, biomedical researcher of Fudan University. He is director of novel coronavirus pneumonia center, director of infectious diseases department of Huashan Hospital Affiliated to Fudan University, Secretary of Party branch, director of Internal Medicine Department of Shanghai Medical College of Fudan University, leader of medical treatment expert group of new crown pneumonia in Shanghai, director of heavy point Laboratory of infectious disease and biosafety emergency response, Shanghai. He has been selected into the new century excellent talent support program of the Ministry of education, is an "excellent discipline leader" in Shanghai and an advanced worker in Shanghai; China's novel coronavirus pneumonia expert was selected as the national health science expert database, the national advanced champion of the new crown pneumonia epidemic situation, the "best teacher in 2020", the "Shanghai science popularization Award for outstanding personages" award, the first "famous doctor of the country" and the "outstanding demeanor" award. In January 31, 2021, Zhang Wenhong selected the "Chinese city character". He won the 2020 Shanghai mayor quality award. In December 2020, Harvest magazine's long special issue 2020 winter volume, the first documentary literary work Dr. Zhang Wenhong; the booklet Dr. Zhang Wenhong was published in August 2021.
