Daokui Li

Daokui Li, Mansfield Freeman Chair Professor, School of Economics and Management, Tsinghua University, Director of China and World Economic Research Center (CCWE), School of Economics and Management, Tsinghua University, Member of the Monetary Policy Committee of the People’s Bank of China, 11th National Committee of the Chinese People’s Political Consultative Conference Member of the session committee. He received a bachelor's degree as the first graduate of the School of Economics and Management of Tsinghua University in 1985, and a PhD in economics from Harvard University in 1992. His main research fields are development economics, corporate finance, international economics, and Chinese economy. Courses taught include Intermediate Microeconomics, Economic Development, International Economics, Chinese Economy, etc.
