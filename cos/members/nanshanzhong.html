Nanshan Zhong

China Nanshan Zhong is Chiness first novel coronavirus pneumonia. The Chinese Communist Party member was born in Nanjing, Jiangsu. She was born in October 20, 1936 in Nanjing, Xiamen, and is a director of respiratory medicine. She is the director of the national respiratory disease clinical medical research center, First Affiliated Hospital of Guangzhou medical University, member of the Chinese Academy of engineering, and member of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences. He used to be the Secretary of the Party committee and President of Guangzhou Medical College (now Guangzhou Medical University), the president of the First Affiliated Hospital, the president of the Chinese Medical Association, the director of the State Key Laboratory of respiratory diseases, the leader of the high-level expert group of the National Health Commission and the National Health Science Popularization expert. Nanshan Zhong was born in a medical family; In August 11, 2020, Xi Jinping signed the presidential decree and awarded the "Medal of Republic" of Zhong Nanshan. On September 4, Zhong Nanshan was selected into the list of "national models of teaching and education" in 2020. In September 3rd, the novel coronavirus pneumonia group was selected to list the expert group. On November 3, Zhong Nanshan was awarded the "science and Technology Achievement Award" of He Liang He Li foundation in 2020. On December 15, he was awarded the honorary doctorate of the Hong Kong Polytechnic University; On December 19, he was awarded the grand lotus Medal of honor issued by the Macao SAR government. Nanshan Zhong has long been committed to the research, prevention and treatment of major respiratory infectious diseases and chronic respiratory diseases, with fruitful results and outstanding achievements.
