Shizhong Fang

Shizhong Fang, male, Han nationality, born in August 1968, a native of Wuhu, Anhui Province, joined the Communist Party of China in January 1989, joined the workforce in July 1990, is a postgraduate student, Doctor of Philosophy and Associate Professor. He is currently the Director of Shanghai Culture and Tourism Bureau. He was the head of the Propaganda Department of the Party Committee of East China Normal University, deputy director of the Propaganda Department of the Municipal Party Committee, machine secretary of the General Office of the Municipal Party Committee, deputy director of the Research Office of the Standing Committee of the Municipal People's Congress, deputy inspector of the Propaganda Department of the Municipal Party Committee, secretary of the Party Committee and president of the Shanghai Grand Theatre Arts Centre and president of the Shanghai Grand Theatre, member of the Standing Committee of the Jing'an District Party Committee and deputy mayor, director and party secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Bureau of Press and Publication and director of the Municipal Copyright Bureau.
