Gongcheng Zheng

Gongcheng Zheng, male, han nationality, born in 1964 at pingkiang, hunan province, renmin university of China social security discipline level 2, professor, doctoral supervisor, school of labor and personnel, renmin university of China, vice dean of the 10th, the 11th and the 12th and 13th session of the standing committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, the 12th vice chairman of the central committee of China democratic league. From September 1981 to July 1985, He studied at Wuhan University and got his bachelor's degree. From July 1985 to May 2000, he worked in Wuhan University as assistant professor, lecturer, associate professor, professor and director of Institute of Social Security, School of Economics. In June 2000, he began to work in Renmin University of China. On December 10, 2017, he was elected vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League. Zheng gongcheng has devoted himself for years to exploring reforms in areas related to people's livelihood, such as the social security system, anti-poverty, employment promotion and philanthropy.
